The Best Movies According to Tim

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Fifth Element(1997)

A must watch! One crazy, action packed,sci-fi/romance movie. Starring Bruce Willis, Milla Jovovich, and Chris Tucker.

Moulin Rouge(2001)

This movie is a true classic and love it. This movie is a dramatic musical, filled with the highs and lows of love. This movie is starred by Ewan McGregor and Nicole Kidman. I challenge anyone to not cry after watching this epic.

The Terminator(1984)

This movie is absolutely brilliant and blew my mind along with many others as a child. This is another action packed sci-fi movie, but with killer future robots and Arnold Schwarzenegger. Another must watch if you are a movie connoisseur!
